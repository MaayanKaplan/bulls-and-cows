Bulls And Cows

How to play?

The goal of the game is to guess a 4 digit number.
Each digit is a number between 1 to 10.
If you got the number and its position right it's called a Bull,
and your number will be replaced by the letter "A".
If you got the number right but in a wrong position it's called a Cow,
and your number will be replaced by the letter "B".
If you got both number and position wrong,
your number will be returned. You have 10 tries.
Let's see if you'll get it!
